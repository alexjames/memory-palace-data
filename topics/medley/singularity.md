### Overton Window
 * Ideas and policies the general public finds politically and socially acceptable at any given time.
 * The stages of any new idea: Unthinkable -> Radical (supported by tiny fraction) -> Acceptable -> Sensible (considered logical by general public) -> Popular -> Policy
 * Politicians generally restrict their platform to whatever the current window dictates.
 * Think tanks, activists, lobby groups, media outlets, and everyday citizens constantly work to shift the window by introducing "radical" or "unthinkable" ideas to normalize them over time.
